Summary Manny (from Replit), and Cong & Denis (from Databricks) walked through our new Replit–Databricks connector, showing how we vibe coded enterprise data apps live. Databricks centralized enterprise data and AI for tens of thousands of customers, so our Replit apps could tap governed tables, models, and warehouses without copying sensitive data around. We used Replit’s Databricks connector to pull real sample datasets into an app, then let the agent vibe code a 3D weather globe that stayed safely inside Databricks.
